About Street Eats

The story behind Haldwani's first street food delivery platform

📖 Our story

Born in Haldwani,
Built for Haldwani

Street Eats was founded in 2026 by four friends from Haldwani who loved street food but wanted a smarter way to enjoy it at home. Late evenings, the craving for hot momos or crispy chowmein would hit — but stepping out wasn't always possible.

We noticed that Zomato and Swiggy only served restaurants. The beloved local momos stall, the chaat corner, the burger thela — all completely ignored by the big platforms. These vendors had loyal customers but zero delivery reach.

So we built Street Eats — Haldwani's own hyperlocal street food delivery platform, made by locals, for locals. Every order you place directly supports a small local vendor and a local delivery partner.

🗺️ Expansion Plan

Our Growth Roadmap

Starting in Haldwani and expanding across Uttarakhand — one city at a time.

April 2026 — Haldwani 🎉

Official launch of Street Eats in Haldwani. First 5+ vendors onboarded. Free delivery for the first month. This is where it all begins.

July 2026 — Rudrapur

Expanding to Rudrapur with a dedicated team. Onboarding local street food vendors and building a delivery network for Udham Singh Nagar.

September 2026 — Ramnagar

Bringing Street Eats to Ramnagar — the gateway to Jim Corbett. Serving both residents and tourists with authentic local street food delivery.

2027 — Nainital & Beyond

Full Kumaon region coverage. Nainital, Bhimtal, Kathgodam and more. Building the most loved local food delivery network in Uttarakhand.
