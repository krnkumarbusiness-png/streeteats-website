✨ Why choose us?

Why Street Eats?

We're not just another food app. We're built specifically for Haldwani's street food culture.

🛺

Local Street Food Only

We only list authentic local street stalls — not restaurants. The real momos wala, the chaat corner, the burger thela. That's Street Eats.

⚡

Fast Delivery

30 minutes or less, every order. We operate daily from 5 PM to 9 PM so you get fresh, hot food exactly when the evening hunger strikes.

💰

Best Price Guaranteed

No hidden charges. No platform fee. Free delivery on every order. You pay exactly what the stall charges — nothing extra, ever.

🤝

Trusted Local Riders

Our delivery partners are known faces from your own neighbourhood. Reliable, punctual, and always friendly. Your order is in good hands.

How It Works

Three steps and your favourite street food is on its way to you.

1
📱
